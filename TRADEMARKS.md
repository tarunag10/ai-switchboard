You may fork and modify the code under the MIT License. Public forks or redistributed builds must use a different app name, bundle identifier, icon, signing identity, and update channel unless permission is granted.

Forks should not imply that they are official releases, endorsed builds, or supported by the maintainers of this repository.

Recommended fork changes:

- Rename the app in the UI and package metadata.
- Use your own bundle identifier.
- Use your own icon and screenshots.
- Disable or replace official update endpoints.
- Sign and notarize releases with your own Apple Developer identity.
